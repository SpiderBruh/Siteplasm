# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Founders and small-to-medium business owners (SMBs) who need high-performance, high-conversion websites delivered at industry-leading speeds (5-day turnaround). They value results, ROI, and directness over agency theater.

## Product Purpose
A marketing portfolio for Siteplasm* agency. It serves as a high-authority sales funnel that proves Siteplasm can ship premium, "money-printing" websites for local and international businesses with clinical precision and zero overhead.

## Brand Personality
**Architectural Authority**
- **Clinical**: Precise, error-free, every element justifies its existence.
- **Velocity**: Fast-moving, result-driven, proof-heavy.
- **Direct**: Zero BS, zero softness, outcome-oriented copy.
- **Aesthetic**: Industrial Minimalism. Black & Gold. Sharp. Authoritative.

## Anti-references
- **❌ Bubbly Consumer Startups**: No soft corners, pastels, or "friendly" vibes.
- **❌ SaaS Dashboard Tropes**: No card-based generic layouts or unnecessary UI toggles.
- **❌ Dark-mode Gamer/Crypto**: No neon, transparency layers, or "posturing" animations.
- **❌ Playful/Fun Vibes**: No gradients, animated blobs, or mascots.
- **❌ Lazy Minimalism**: No empty white space used as a cop-out.

## Design Principles
1. **Structural Justification**: Every margin, border, and alignment must be intentional and structural.
2. **Outcome-First Proof**: Design serves to highlight ROI, speed, and real-world results (Proof over Promises).
3. **Industrial Craft**: Use raw, sharp, and high-contrast elements to signal architectural quality.
4. **Cinematic Precision**: Animations must be subtle, fast, and purely functional to prove shipped velocity.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance**: High-contrast ratios (Black & Gold) are non-negotiable.
- **Performance First**: Zero layout shifts, optimized for mobile-first SMB owners.
- **Focus Clarity**: Sharp, visible focus states for all interactive elements.

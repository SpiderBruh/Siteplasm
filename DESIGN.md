# Siteplasm* Design System

## 1. Brand Identity
**Concept**: "Zero BS, Maximum Impact." Siteplasm is a high-performance agency that prioritizes speed-to-market and high conversion over fluffy design.
**Keywords**: Kinetic, Brutalist-Premium, High-Contrast, Professional.

## 2. Color Palette
- **Primary (Background)**: `#000000` (Pitch Black)
- **Secondary (Text)**: `#FFFFFF` (Pure White)
- **Accent**: `#FF3E00` (Electric Orange - representing energy and speed)
- **Muted**: `#1A1A1A` (Deep Grey for cards and sections)
- **Success**: `#00FF41` (Matrix Green for conversion signals)

## 3. Typography
- **Display (Headlines)**: `Syne` (Bold, Wide, Aggressive) or `Bebas Neue`.
- **Body**: `DM Sans` (Clean, Modern, Readable).
- **Mono**: `JetBrains Mono` (For technical specs and "behind-the-scenes" feel).

## 4. Layout & Spacing
- **Grid**: 12-column fluid grid.
- **Max Width**: `1440px`.
- **Spacing Scale**: 4px base (4, 8, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96, 128).
- **Hero Density**: High (96px - 128px padding).

## 5. Components
- **Buttons**: Sharp corners, heavy borders, kinetic hover (lifts or glows).
- **Cards**: Minimal borders (`1px solid #1A1A1A`), subtle grain overlay.
- **Motion**: 1.5s duration smooth scrolling (Lenis), Framer Motion for entry reveals.

## 6. Depth & Elevation
- **Z-Index**: Grain overlay at top, fixed nav below.
- **Shadows**: None (Flat design with high contrast).

## 7. Responsive Behavior
- **Desktop**: 1440px
- **Tablet**: 768px (Stacks grid to 2 columns)
- **Mobile**: 375px (Single column, fluid typography).
